Membership Options

Casual - $25.99

You're choosing to begin a habit that will positively effect your life as long as you stay consistent. This is our most price friendly option, and it's for those who either do or don't have what it takes to make this a lifestyle. It's up to you.

*Price may vary at time of purchase

Serious - $35.99

Going to the gym is part of your daily routine. You're the type of person who, if they don't work out, feel out of equilibrium and you likely hold yourself to a higher standard than your peers when it comes to physique as well as Health & Wellness in general.

This membership includes the following:
- Access to Calisthenetics room
- Access to Sauna
- Unlimited Guest privileges (1 max.)

*Price may vary at time of purchase

Professional - $45.99

This is what you do. We have access to trainers who hold awards at the state level to help you on your journey to becoming the best you can be. This membership includes everything the serious membership does, as well as access to monthly personal mentorship from one of our top trainers who volunteer their time to be with people like you.

*Price may vary at time of purchase
